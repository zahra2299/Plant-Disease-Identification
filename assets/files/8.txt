
Common Name: Basil
Scientific Name: Ocimum basilicum
Family: Lamiaceae
Cultivation: Full sun, well-draining soil, regular watering.
Propagation: Seeds, cuttings.
Planting: After the last frost, 8-12 inches apart.
Care: Pinch off flower buds for better leaf production, regular harvesting encourages growth.
Harvesting: Leaves can be harvested as needed, avoid cutting more than one-third of the plant at a time.
