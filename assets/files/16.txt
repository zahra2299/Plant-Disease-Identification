
Common Name: Peace Lily
Scientific Name: Spathiphyllum spp.
Family: Araceae

Cultivation: Indirect sunlight to moderate shade, well-draining soil. Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.

Propagation: Peace Lily can be propagated by division of clumps or by root division.

Planting: Plant Peace Lily in containers with drainage holes, using a well-draining potting mix.

Care: Wipe the leaves regularly with a damp cloth to remove dust and maintain foliage health. Avoid exposing Peace Lily to cold drafts or temperature extremes.

Maintenance: Peace Lily will benefit from occasional repotting and division to rejuvenate overcrowded plants and promote flowering.
