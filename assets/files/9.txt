
Common Name: Lavender
Scientific Name: Lavandula
Family: Lamiaceae
Cultivation: Full sun, well-draining soil, minimal watering once established.
Propagation: Seeds, cuttings.
Planting: Spring or fall, 12-24 inches apart.
Care: Prune after flowering to maintain shape, avoid over-watering.
Harvesting: Flowers can be harvested when fully open for culinary or decorative use.
