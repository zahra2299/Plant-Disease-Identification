
Common Name: Rosemary
Scientific Name: Rosmarinus officinalis
Family: Lamiaceae
Cultivation: Full sun, well-draining soil, drought-tolerant once established.
Propagation: Stem cuttings.
Planting: Spring, 18-24 inches apart.
Care: Prune to maintain shape, avoid over-watering.
Harvesting: Can be harvested as needed, fresh or dried leaves are used in cooking.
