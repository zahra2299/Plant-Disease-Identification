
Common Name: Mint
Scientific Name: Mentha
Family: Lamiaceae
Cultivation: Partial shade to full sun, moist soil.
Propagation: Stem cuttings, division.
Planting: Spring, 12-18 inches apart.
Care: Regular pruning to prevent legginess, can become invasive, so consider planting in containers.
Harvesting: Leaves can be harvested as needed for culinary use.
